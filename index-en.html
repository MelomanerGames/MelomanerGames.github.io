How I Work

I am most useful when a team needs to turn scattered ideas, constraints, and metrics into a clear action plan. I identify issues quickly, align stakeholders, move tasks to release, and keep the product focused on player value.

My preferred mode is producer-minded: balancing game quality, business goals, and team capacity, then validating decisions through data, tests, and the product's post-release state.
