About Me

I am a Producer in game development, leading projects from concept and prototype to release and live support. I have worked on mobile, web, VR/LBE VR, and online products. In practice, I also cover Project Manager and Game Designer responsibilities: prioritizing backlog, planning roadmap and sprints, and coordinating the team. I make product decisions through data: funnel analysis, hypothesis validation, monetization and balance testing, A/B experiments, and pre-release build checks.
