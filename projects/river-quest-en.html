Obby & Dead River

Status: Released

Platforms: Yandex Games | CrazyGames

A first-person 3D Roblox-style game: fight monsters, travel along the Dead River, explore castles and villages, collect loot, and defeat the final boss.

About the Game

The player follows a dangerous route along the legendary Dead River that connects major castles. During the journey, you fight monsters, pirates, and dragons to reach the final boss.

In villages you can trade, collect treasure, upgrade weapons, and prepare for the next route segment. Each new castle increases difficulty, but rewards become more valuable.

What I Did on the Project

  • Monetization: designed and implemented monetization mechanics
  • Saves: configured the progress saving system
  • Progression: implemented player upgrade mechanics
  • Advertising: configured ad format placements
  • Balance: balanced economy and combat parameters

Key Features

  • A Dead River journey through a chain of castles
  • Battles against monsters, pirates, dragons, and a final boss
  • Loot collection, treasures, and trading in villages
  • Weapon upgrades, medkit purchases, and preparation for new runs
  • First-person view with a Roblox-inspired visual style
